Camila Rezende

Has held post-doctoral positions at LLB/Saclay, at the Institute of Chemistry at UNICAMP, and at the Institute of Physics of São Carlos of the University of São Paulo. Worked as a visitant researcher at University of York (CNAP, 2017).
